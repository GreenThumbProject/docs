# References

Academic references and resources used in the GreenThumb project.

## Core References

### Hydroponics and IoT

1. **Boonnam, N. et al. (2017)**
   - *Optimal Plant Growth in Smart Farm Hydroponics System using the Integration of Wireless Sensor Networks into Internet of Things*
   - Advances in Science, Technology and Engineering Systems Journal, vol. 2, no. 3, p. 1006-1012
   - [DOI: 10.25046/aj0203127](https://doi.org/10.25046/aj0203127)

2. **Nnadi, S., Idachaba, F. (2018)**
   - *Design and Implementation of a Sustainable IoT Enabled Greenhouse Prototype*
   - IEEE 5G World Forum
   - [DOI: 10.1109/5gwf.2018.8517006](https://doi.org/10.1109/5gwf.2018.8517006)

3. **Gokul, S. et al. (2021)**
   - *Smart Hydroponic System Using IoT*
   - Proceedings of ICICC 2021
   - [DOI: 10.2139/ssrn.3884824](http://dx.doi.org/10.2139/ssrn.3884824)

4. **Tatas, K. et al. (2022)**
   - *Reliable IoT-Based Monitoring and Control of Hydroponic Systems*
   - Technologies, 10, 26
   - [DOI: 10.3390/technologies10010026](https://doi.org/10.3390/technologies10010026)

### Computer Vision and ML

5. **Nesteruk, S. et al. (2020)**
   - *Plant Growth Prediction through Intelligent Embedded Sensing*
   - IEEE 29th International Symposium on Industrial Electronics
   - [DOI: 10.1109/isie45063.2020.9152399](https://doi.org/10.1109/isie45063.2020.9152399)

6. **Al Rasyid, M., Mufid, M. (2023)**
   - *Vision-Based Object Detection for Efficient Monitoring in Smart Hydroponic Systems*
   - Proceedings of iCAST-ES 2023, AMER 230
   - [DOI: 10.2991/978-94-6463-364-1_40](https://doi.org/10.2991/978-94-6463-364-1_40)

### Plant Nutrition

7. **Dhal, S.B. et al. (2022)**
   - *Nutrient optimization for plant growth in Aquaponic irrigation using machine learning for small training datasets*
   - Artificial Intelligence in Agriculture, 6, 68-76
   - Key finding: ML optimization reduced plant growth time
